Games


Zork

Games68.com

In the OASIS, on the planet Frobozz, using a 3D recreation of the text adventure, an avatar must collect all 20 trophies, put them in the trophy case, and blow a whistle found in a Cap'n Crunch cereal box to obtain the Jade key.

Joust


In order to obtain the Copper Key, an OASIS avatar must defeat Acererak the Demi-Lich in a best-of-three match of Joust.


Pac-Man


On Archaide, Parzival performed a perfect game on a Pac-Man machine that had been marked as Out of Order. He was rewarded with the 1981 Quarter Artifact.

To complete the Second Gate, an OASIS avatar must complete a full recreation of the game from beginning to end. Upon completion of the game, the avatar will be able to select one of several robots to be used in the OASIS, such as Gigantor, Tranzor Z, the Iron Giant, Jet Jaguar, Giant Robo from Johnny Sokko and his Flying Robot, robots from the Shogun Warriors, Macross, and Gundam franchises, Leopardon, Mechagodzilla, and Raideen.
